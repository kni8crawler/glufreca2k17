Glufreca-2k16

Glufreca-2k16 was the 1st regional camp organized by GLUE in association with FSMK and Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering in Web Development domain. It was a super successful 4 days camp which had around 90+ participants and 15+ volunteers from GECH, MCE, NDRKIT and RIT. We had 6+ efficient and effective resource persons from FSMK who were with us throughout the camp by making time from their tight schedule. All resource persons from FSMK were at their best in reaching out participants and keep hold of their attention throughout their session and volunteers also gave their fullest to help the participants to understand the concepts very clearly. Along with the same moment, passion and energy GLUE is set to host Glufreca-2k17 with even more energy and enthusiasm.
